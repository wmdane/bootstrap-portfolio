About Me

I was born September 26, 1994 in Decatur, Georgia. I moved to Charlotte, NC in 1997 and remained there throughout my K-12 years. I attended the University of North Carolina at Greensboro from 2012-2016 where I earned a bachelors degree in International Studies. After graduating, I moved back to Charlotte and worked various positions at Jimmy Johns while searching for a more stable career. During this time I also earned a certification to teach English as a foreign language with plans to use it to teach English abroad.

Currently, I am taking a full-stack web development bootcamp in the hopes that I can get a good grounding in various web development tools and use the skills I learn to transfer into a more lucrative and competetive field.
